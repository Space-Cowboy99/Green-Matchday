# Roadmap (draft)

**Phase 1 — Open Core**
- Calculator engine (Python), CLI, seed datasets, tests, MIT, docs.

**Phase 2 — Demo Web App**
- Streamlit page: Clubs (fixture picker) and Fans (trip calc), CSV export.

**Phase 3 — Club Workflow**
- Bulk upload (fixtures.csv) → results, club presets, methodology & changelog.

**Phase 4 — Reporting**
- One-page PDF summary, “what-if” mode-share scenarios.

**Phase 5 — Scale & Community**
- More leagues, country factors, CI data checks.
